Crystal Clear Pool

Experience our state-of-the-art Olympic-sized swimming pool with crystal clear water maintained through our filtration system. We perform scheduled water changes and chemical balancing to ensure maximum hygiene and safety for all swimmers.

  • Temperature-controlled water (30°C)
  • Advanced filtration system
  • Lifeguard supervision

Our premium gym facility is located on the second floor, offering panoramic views of the pool below. Equipped with the latest fitness equipment from top brands, our gym provides everything you need for a complete workout experience.

  • Personal training available
